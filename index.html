How To Play MAGNEX

1

Turn-Based Placement

Players take turns placing a single magnet on any empty spot inside the circular tabletop arena. All placed stones are visually monochrome neon-white metallic spheres.

2

Magnetic Pull (Physics)

All placed stones slide freely. When the distance between any two is within the attraction circle (150px threshold), they begin pulling each other together following the Inverse-Square Law.

3

Snap Penalty

When stones pull together and collide, they instantly snap! Triggering a snap removes all colliding stones from the board, adds them to your snapped count (Hane), and deducts 1 Life (Can).

4

Lives & Win Conditions

Each player starts with 5 Lives (Can) and an unlimited pool of stones. The first player whose lives drop to 0 loses instantly, crowning the other player as the victor!
